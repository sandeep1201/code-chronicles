# Trade Plan Development

## Description

Creates a complete, actionable trade plan with entry zones, stop loss, profit
targets, and position sizing.

## Prompt

Create a complete trade plan for [STOCK_NAME/TICKER].

Include:

- Trade thesis (why this trade makes sense)
- Ideal entry zones (primary and secondary)
- Stop loss placement with reasoning
- Multiple profit targets (TP1, TP2, TP3) with probabilities
- Position sizing based on low-risk strategy (risk per trade as % of capital)
- Trade management rules (trailing stops, partial exits, etc.)
- Risk-reward ratio calculation
- Expected holding period
- Conditions for early exit or adjustment

## Usage

Replace [STOCK_NAME/TICKER] with the actual stock symbol. Use this skill after
completing individual stock analysis to create a detailed, executable trade plan
before entering any position.
