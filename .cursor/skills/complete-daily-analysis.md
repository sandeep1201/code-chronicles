# Complete Daily Market Analysis

## Description

Orchestrates all market analysis skills in a comprehensive workflow for complete
daily market assessment.

## Prompt

Perform a complete daily market analysis using the following workflow:

**Step 1: Market Regime Analysis** Determine the current market regime: trending
(bullish/bearish), ranging, risk-on, or risk-off.

- Evidence supporting the regime classification
- How trading strategies should adapt in this environment
- Common mistakes traders usually make in this regime
- Key indicators to watch for regime changes

**Step 2: Daily Market Overview** Scan today's global markets and summarize the
top 5 forces moving stocks right now.

- Macro events (economic data, central bank policy, geopolitical developments)
- Sector rotation patterns
- Market sentiment shifts
- Key technical levels being tested
- Explain what matters and what is noise

**Step 3: Stock Opportunity Identification** Based on current market conditions,
generate a shortlist of high-probability stocks for the next 30 days. For each
stock, provide:

- Ticker symbol and company name
- Primary reason why this stock stands out
- Key risk factors
- Specific conditions that would invalidate the trade
- Recommended time horizon

**Step 4: Deep Dive Analysis (for top 3 opportunities)** For the top 3 stocks
from Step 3, provide:

- Technical structure analysis
- Fundamentals review
- Recent news and catalysts
- Bullish/bearish bias with confidence level
- Key price levels to watch
- Trader type suitability

**Step 5: Trade Plan Development (for selected stocks)** For stocks you plan to
trade, create complete trade plans with:

- Trade thesis
- Entry zones (primary and secondary)
- Stop loss placement
- Multiple profit targets with probabilities
- Position sizing based on risk management
- Trade management rules
- Risk-reward ratio

**Step 6: Earnings Calendar Review** Identify any upcoming earnings in the next
2 weeks that may impact your watchlist or positions. For each:

- Historical earnings reaction pattern
- Current sentiment and positioning
- Expected move scenarios
- Risk factors

**Step 7: Portfolio Health Check** Review current portfolio (if applicable):

- Alignment with current market regime
- Sector allocation balance
- Risk exposure
- Rebalancing needs

**Output Format:** Present the analysis in a clear, structured format with
actionable insights prioritized. Use sections and bullet points for easy
scanning. Highlight any urgent items or time-sensitive opportunities.

## Usage

Use this master skill for comprehensive daily market analysis. This orchestrates
all individual skills in the optimal sequence for complete market assessment.
You can also use individual skills separately for focused analysis on specific
areas.

## Workflow Sequence

1. Market Regime Analysis → Understand environment
2. Daily Market Overview → Get current context
3. Stock Opportunity Identification → Find potential trades
4. Individual Stock Analysis → Deep dive on opportunities
5. Trade Plan Development → Plan your entries
6. Earnings Analysis → Prepare for catalysts
7. Portfolio Construction/Review → Maintain diversification
8. Trade Review → Learn and improve (weekly)
