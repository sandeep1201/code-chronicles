# Trade Review & Performance Analysis

## Description

Conducts a comprehensive post-mortem analysis of completed trades to identify
mistakes, strengths, and areas for improvement.

## Prompt

Review this completed trade: [TRADE_DETAILS - include entry, exit, duration,
P&L, original thesis]

Conduct a post-mortem analysis covering:

- Execution quality (entry/exit timing)
- Trade thesis accuracy (what worked, what didn't)
- Mistakes made (entry too early/late, stop too tight/wide, etc.)
- Strengths demonstrated
- Psychological biases that may have influenced the trade (FOMO, revenge
  trading, fear of missing gains, etc.)
- Risk management effectiveness
- Market context alignment

Recommendations:

- What should be done differently next time
- What worked well and should be repeated
- Key learnings to incorporate into future trades
- Process improvements for long-term performance

## Usage

Replace [TRADE_DETAILS] with complete trade information including entry price,
exit price, duration, P&L, and your original trading thesis. Use this skill
after closing trades to continuously improve your trading process.
