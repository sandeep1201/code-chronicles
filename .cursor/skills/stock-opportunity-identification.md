# Stock Opportunity Identification

## Description

Generates a shortlist of high-probability stocks for the next 30 days based on
current market conditions.

## Prompt

Based on current market conditions, generate a shortlist of high-probability
stocks for the next 30 days.

For each stock, provide:

- Ticker symbol and company name
- Primary reason why this stock stands out (technical, fundamental, or
  catalyst-driven)
- Key risk factors
- Specific conditions that would invalidate the trade
- Recommended time horizon (short-term swing, position trade, etc.)

## Usage

Use this skill after understanding the market regime to identify potential
trading opportunities. This helps you focus your research on the most promising
stocks.
