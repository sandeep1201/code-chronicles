# Daily Market Overview

## Description

Scans today's global markets and summarizes the top 5 forces moving stocks right
now, focusing on macro events, sector rotation, and sentiment shifts.

## Prompt

Scan today's global markets and summarize the top 5 forces moving stocks right
now. Focus on:

- Macro events (economic data, central bank policy, geopolitical developments)
- Sector rotation patterns
- Market sentiment shifts
- Key technical levels being tested

Explain what matters and what is noise. Prioritize actionable insights over
background information.

## Usage

Use this skill at the start of your daily market analysis to get current market
context and identify the most important drivers of price action.
